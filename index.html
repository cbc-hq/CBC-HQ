How to download materials
Follow these simple steps to access any study material on CBC HQ — it only takes a minute!
1

Browse & find

Use the level tabs (Primary, Junior, Senior) and type tabs (Notes, CATs, Exams) to find the material you need.

2

Click Download PDF

Click the green "Download PDF" button on any resource card. A popup will appear before the download begins.

3

Subscribe & download

Click "Subscribe on YouTube & Download" — your YouTube channel opens to subscribe, and your document downloads automatically!
